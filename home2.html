Why NewLeaf exists.

Born from a deep belief that grief deserves more than a diagnosis.

NewLeaf was founded in 2009 after Dr. Sarah Jenkins witnessed how little structured, compassionate support existed for those navigating bereavement outside of clinical settings. She set out to build something different — a practice that felt less like a waiting room and more like a sanctuary.

Today, NewLeaf brings together a small, carefully chosen team of grief specialists who share one conviction: that healing is not about moving on, but about learning to carry your loss with grace.

How It Works

Taking the first step is the hardest part. We have made the rest as simple and gentle as possible.

01

Reach Out

Send us a brief enquiry through our contact form — no lengthy forms, no clinical intake paperwork. Just a few words about where you are right now. We will respond within one working day, gently and without pressure.

Start with an enquiry
02

A Gentle Introduction

We arrange a free 20-minute introductory call so you can speak with one of our therapists before committing to anything. This is simply a conversation — a chance to ask questions, share a little, and feel whether the fit is right for you.

03

Begin Your First Session

When you feel ready, we book your first session — in person at our practice or online from the comfort of your home. There is no expectation, no agenda. Just a safe, unhurried space that is entirely yours.
