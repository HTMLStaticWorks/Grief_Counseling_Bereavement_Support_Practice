Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about beginning therapy with us.

Your first session is primarily about building a sense of safety and trust. We will go at your pace, allowing you to share as much or as little as you feel comfortable with. It's a time for us to get to know one another and discuss your hopes for therapy.

Grief has no set timeline. The duration of therapy varies greatly depending on individual needs and circumstances. Some clients find comfort in a few months of support, while others prefer ongoing, periodic check-ins over a longer period.
